Tickets & Pricing

Visitors can choose from various ticket options based on their preferred experience. Standard admission grants access to the second floor, while summit tickets provide access to the top of the tower for a breathtaking view of Paris. Discounts are available for children, students, and seniors. Skip-the-line tickets and guided tour packages are also available. Booking online is highly recommended, especially during peak tourist seasons.
