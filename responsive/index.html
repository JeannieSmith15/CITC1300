The Statue of Liberty – A Symbol of Freedom

Welcome to the Statue of Liberty, one of the most iconic landmarks in the world! Standing proudly in New York Harbor, this monument has been a beacon of hope and freedom for over a centuryFor over 135 years, the Statue of Liberty has stood as a universal symbol of freedom, democracy, and opportunity. This magnificent monument, located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, has welcomed generations of immigrants and visitors from around the world..

A Beacon of Freedom

More than just a statue, Lady Liberty represents the ideals of justice and liberty—values that define the United States. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a first-time visitor, or a seasoned traveler, exploring the Statue of Liberty is an experience that stays with you forever.

Interesting Facts

  • The full name of the statue is Liberty Enlightening the World.
  • It was transported in 350 pieces and reassembled in New York.
  • The crown has seven spikes, representing the seven continents and seas.

Plan Your Visit

  • Location: Liberty Island, New York Harbor
  • How to Get There: Take a ferry from Battery Park (NYC) or Liberty State Park (NJ)
  • Tickets & Tours: Options include pedestal access and crown visits (advance reservations required).
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring and fall offer the best weather with fewer crowds.
